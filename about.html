About Awoyaa

Revolutionizing business management through innovative multi-business ERP solutions

Empowering Businesses to Scale Without Limits

Ayowaa was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create an enterprise resource planning solution that grows with your business, no matter how many ventures you pursue or locations you operate.

Founded in 2020 by a team of experienced entrepreneurs and software engineers, Awoyaa addresses the unique challenges faced by multi-business owners, franchise operators, and growing enterprises who need to manage diverse operations under one unified platform.

Today, we serve over 500+ businesses worldwide, from small family-owned enterprises to large multi-location corporations, helping them streamline operations, reduce costs, and accelerate growth through our innovative cloud-based ERP platform.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade business management tools by providing an intuitive, scalable, and affordable ERP solution that enables businesses of all sizes to operate multiple ventures efficiently and profitably.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading multi-business ERP platform, empowering entrepreneurs and enterprises to seamlessly manage unlimited businesses, locations, and operations from a single, unified dashboard.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to meet changing business needs
  • Simplicity: Making complex business management intuitive and accessible
  • Reliability: Providing dependable solutions businesses can trust
  • Growth: Enabling our customers' success and expansion
  • Transparency: Building trust through honest communication and fair pricing

What We Do

Comprehensive business management solutions designed for the modern entrepreneur

Multi-Business Management

Seamlessly manage unlimited businesses from a single platform. Each business operates independently with its own inventory, customers, suppliers, and financial records, while providing consolidated reporting and oversight.

  • Independent business configurations
  • Consolidated dashboard views
  • Cross-business reporting
  • Unified user management

Inventory Management

Advanced inventory tracking across multiple locations with real-time stock levels, automated reorder points, and comprehensive product management including variants, bundles, and services.

  • Multi-location inventory tracking
  • Automated stock alerts
  • Product variants and bundles
  • Barcode scanning support

Point of Sale (POS)

Modern, touch-friendly POS system with support for multiple payment methods, custom receipts, and seamless integration with inventory and accounting modules for real-time business insights.

  • Touch-optimized interface
  • Multiple payment methods
  • Custom receipt templates
  • Offline mode capability

Financial Management

Complete accounting suite with automated bookkeeping, financial reporting, tax management, and multi-currency support. Track profitability across all your business ventures with detailed financial insights.

  • Automated bookkeeping
  • Multi-currency support
  • Tax management
  • Financial reporting

Customer Relationship Management

Comprehensive CRM tools to manage customer relationships, track sales pipelines, automate follow-ups, and provide exceptional customer service across all your business locations.

  • Customer lifecycle management
  • Sales pipeline tracking
  • Automated communications
  • Customer support ticketing

Analytics & Reporting

Powerful business intelligence tools with customizable dashboards, real-time analytics, and comprehensive reporting across all business functions to drive data-driven decision making.

  • Real-time dashboards
  • Custom report builder
  • Performance analytics
  • Predictive insights
