Your Role

As an Awoyaa Agent or Partner, you will make a real difference in your community

Promote & Market

Promote and market our software solutions Awoyaa to businesses in your area.

Setup & Onboard

Set up and onboard businesses onto our applications, ensuring they understand how to use the software effectively.

Data Entry

Perform data/product entry into the applications to ensure businesses are ready to operate efficiently from day one.

Provide Support

Offer remote and in-person support to business owners, helping them maximize the value of our software solutions.

Conduct Audits

Conduct regular and random audits to ensure system compliance and usage, maintaining quality standards across all implementations.

Continuous Learning

Continuously study and learn about new and existing software solutions to better support customers and stay ahead of technology trends.

Compensation Structure

Awoyaa Partners are commission-based agents who earn based on their performance and the value they bring to businesses.

Setup Fee Commission

Earn a percentage of each business setup fee when you successfully onboard a new client to our platform.

Recurring Monthly Commission

Receive ongoing monthly commissions based on the businesses you onboard and continue to support, creating a sustainable income stream.

Additional Benefits

  • Performance bonuses for exceeding targets
  • Incentives for customer retention
  • Training and certification programs
  • Recognition and awards for top performers
  • Opportunities for career advancement

Job Requirements

We don't need your certificates; we need your integrity and drive.

To qualify, you must:

Basic Computer Knowledge

Have basic to good computer knowledge including using apps, typing, and internet usage. No advanced technical skills required.

Integrity & Drive

Be honest, responsible, and self-motivated. We value character and work ethic above formal qualifications.

Willingness to Learn

Be willing to learn quickly and follow up with businesses professionally. We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support.

Digital Proficiency

Be confident using digital forms, spreadsheets, and basic data entry tools. Comfort with mobile apps and online platforms is essential.

Communication Skills

Have good communication and people skills. Ability to explain technical concepts in simple terms and build relationships with business owners.

Team Player

Be able to work independently or in a team. Flexibility to adapt to different working styles and collaborate effectively with colleagues.
