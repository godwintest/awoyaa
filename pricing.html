Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common pricing questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your plan limits. If you exceed your limits, your account will continue to function, but we'll recommend upgrading to a more suitable plan. We won't automatically charge you for overages.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount when you pay annually instead of monthly. You can select annual billing during signup or switch from the billing settings in your account.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans with no credit card required. You can try all the features of your selected plan during the trial period before making a purchase decision.
